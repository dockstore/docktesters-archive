>
>
> *Comparison ----------*
> Total original (dkfz): 16090
> Total this: 51087
> *Common: 16090*

> Whit means that in the original VCF there are 16K mutations, all of them

> #CHROM  POS     ID      REF     ALT     QUAL    FILTER  INFO    FORMAT
> CONTROL TUMOR
> 1       725971  .       G       T       .       RE;BL;TAC;HSDEPTH;SBAF;FRQ;VAF

> 0/0:114:49,62,0,3
